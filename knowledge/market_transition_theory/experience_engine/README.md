# MarketMirrorAI Experience Engine



Purpose:



Store and analyze real-world outcomes after MarketMirrorAI generates a diagnosis.



Books provide theoretical knowledge.



Experience provides operational knowledge.



Core Principle:



Every signal becomes a future learning opportunity.



Process:



Diagnosis

↓

Decision

↓

Outcome

↓

Review

↓

Lesson

↓

Knowledge Update



Objective:



Reduce repeated mistakes.



Increase diagnostic precision.



Improve future confidence estimates.



Important Rule:



A successful trade does not automatically validate a diagnosis.



A failed trade does not automatically invalidate a diagnosis.



Root-cause analysis is required.
