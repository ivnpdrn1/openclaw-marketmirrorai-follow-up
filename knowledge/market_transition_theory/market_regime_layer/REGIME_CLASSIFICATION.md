# Market Regime Classification



Purpose:



Define how MarketMirrorAI classifies the current market regime before pattern evaluation.



Core Principle:



Market regime influences confidence, pattern activation, and execution discipline.



Market regime never replaces visual diagnosis.



Visual diagnosis remains primary.



---



## Bull Market



Characteristics:



* SPY above MA50

* SPY above MA200

* QQQ above MA50

* QQQ above MA200

* Higher highs

* Higher lows

* Strong bullish breadth



Visual Indicators:



* Uptrend structure

* Repeated support holds

* Bullish continuation patterns



Effect:



Increase confidence in CALL setups.



Reduce confidence in PUT setups.



---



## Bear Market



Characteristics:



* SPY below MA50

* SPY below MA200

* QQQ below MA50

* QQQ below MA200

* Lower highs

* Lower lows

* Persistent selling pressure



Visual Indicators:



* Descending channels

* Failed rallies

* Repeated support failures



Effect:



Increase confidence in PUT setups.



Reduce confidence in CALL setups.



---



## Sideways Market



Characteristics:



* Price trapped inside range

* No clear trend

* Repeated support and resistance tests



Visual Indicators:



* Horizontal structure

* Frequent reversals

* Mixed signals



Effect:



Reduce confidence for all directional setups.



Increase WAIT probability.



---



## High Volatility



Characteristics:



* Large candles

* Large gaps

* Wide intraday ranges

* Elevated VIX



Visual Indicators:



* Explosive movement

* Large candle bodies

* Fast trend changes



Effect:



Increase risk.



Require stronger confirmation.



Increase WAIT threshold.



---



## Low Volatility



Characteristics:



* Small candles

* Tight ranges

* Low VIX



Visual Indicators:



* Compression

* Consolidation

* Weak directional movement



Effect:



Reduce confidence.



Expect slower movement.



---



## Risk-On



Characteristics:



* Growth stocks strong

* QQQ outperforming SPY

* Technology leadership

* Bullish market participation



Visual Indicators:



* Strong breakouts

* Strong momentum

* Expanding volume



Effect:



Increase confidence in bullish continuation.



---



## Risk-Off



Characteristics:



* Defensive rotation

* Technology weakness

* Flight to safety

* Broad selling pressure



Visual Indicators:



* Failed breakouts

* Gap downs

* Defensive behavior



Effect:



Increase confidence in bearish continuation.



Reduce confidence in bullish setups.



---

---



## Conflict Rule



If Bull Market and Risk-Off appear simultaneously:



Regime Status:



Mixed



Required Action:



Reduce confidence.



Increase confirmation requirements.



Prefer WAIT.



---



## Operational Rule



Every diagnosis must include:



* Market Regime

* Regime Confidence

* Regime Evidence



No CALL or PUT recommendation should be produced without regime classification.
