# MarketMirrorAI Trade Lifecycle Analytics



Purpose:



Measure the quality of every completed BUY-SELL cycle.



Core Principle:



A diagnosis is not complete until the full market cycle has ended.



MarketMirrorAI must evaluate:



- entry anticipation

- real trend duration

- exit anticipation

- captured movement

- missed movement

- decision quality



Core Metrics:



- t1

- t2

- t3

- h

- Capture Efficiency

- Ratio1

- Ratio2



Definitions:



t1:



Time between BUY Decision Point and actual trend change.



Larger is better.



t2:



Time between actual trend change and actual opposite trend change.



Larger is better.



t3:



Time between SELL Decision Point and actual opposite trend change.



Larger is better.



h:



Total economic height of the trend.



Larger is better.



Operational Sequence:



BUY Decision Point

↓

Actual Trend Change

↓

Trend Development

↓

SELL Decision Point

↓

Actual Opposite Trend Change

↓

Trade Review

↓

Knowledge Update

↓

Future Improvement
