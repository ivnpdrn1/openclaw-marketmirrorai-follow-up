# MMAI Visual Signatures



## MMAI-001

Name:

Piso Fuerte SPY



Visual Signature:

Price approaches or touches a strong support floor and rejects lower prices.



Main Visual Elements:

- support zone

- repeated defense of price

- decreasing selling pressure

- possible bullish reversal candle

- SPY context



---



## MMAI-002

Name:

Primer Gap al Alza



Visual Signature:

After a significant decline near a strong floor, price opens with a bullish gap and the gap floor holds.



Main Visual Elements:

- prior decline

- strong floor nearby

- bullish gap

- first green candle

- gap floor respected

- hourly timeframe

- volume confirmation

## MMAI-005

Name:

Canal Bajista



Visual Signature:

Price moves inside a downward channel with lower highs and lower lows.



Main Visual Elements:

- descending channel

- lower highs

- lower lows

- failed rallies

- bearish continuation



---



## MMAI-007

Name:

Primera Vela Roja de Apertura



Visual Signature:

Market opens and the first candle shows immediate bearish pressure.



Main Visual Elements:

- opening candle

- red candle

- selling pressure

- volume expansion

- weak continuation attempt



---



## MMAI-008

Name:

Ruptura del Piso del Gap



Visual Signature:

A previous gap floor fails and price breaks below it.



Main Visual Elements:

- prior gap

- visible gap floor

- breakdown below floor

- selling pressure

- failed support
