# MMAI Pattern Dependencies



Purpose:



Define relationships between MMAI patterns.



Patterns are not always independent.



Some patterns act as primary diagnosis.



Other patterns act as confirmation.



---



## MMAI-001 -> MMAI-002



Primary Pattern:



MMAI-001

Piso Fuerte SPY



Confirmation Pattern:



MMAI-002

Primer Gap al Alza



Relationship:



MMAI-001 can generate a CALL opportunity by itself when all rules are satisfied.



MMAI-002 often acts as a powerful confirmation after MMAI-001.



Operational Meaning:



Piso Fuerte identifies the opportunity zone.



Primer Gap al Alza confirms bullish transition.



Decision Rule:



If MMAI-001 is valid and MMAI-002 appears, CALL confidence increases.



If MMAI-001 is valid but MMAI-002 does not appear, CALL may still be possible if hourly confirmation, green candle, and volume are present.



If MMAI-002 appears without a valid Piso Fuerte or support context, confidence must be reduced.

Active
