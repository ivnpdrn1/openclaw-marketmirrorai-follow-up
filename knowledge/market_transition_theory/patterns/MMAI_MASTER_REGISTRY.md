## MMAI-005



Name:



Canal Bajista



Category:



Market Structure



Signal:



WAIT



Primary Outcome:



Await Breakout



Visual Required:



Yes



Status:



Book Extracted



Operational Role:



Transition Structure



Description:



A Canal Bajista is not itself a CALL setup.



It represents a market condition where buying inside the channel is discouraged.



The preferred action is to wait for a breakout of the channel ceiling.



The breakout may later contribute to MMAI-001 and MMAI-002 development.
