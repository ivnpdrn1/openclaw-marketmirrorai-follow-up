# WAIT Decision Tree



Purpose:



Define when MarketMirrorAI must avoid action.



---



WAIT Conditions:



- Visual pattern is unclear.

- Multiple patterns conflict.

- Rules are incomplete.

- Volume does not confirm.

- Timeframes disagree.

- SPY context contradicts the signal.

- Major catalyst risk is active.

- Confidence score is below execution threshold.

- Book evidence does not match live chart.

- Risk/reward is not favorable.



---



Operational Rule:



WAIT is not a failure.



WAIT is the correct decision when evidence is insufficient.



---



Final Decision



WAIT



When uncertainty is high, protection of capital has priority over opportunity.
