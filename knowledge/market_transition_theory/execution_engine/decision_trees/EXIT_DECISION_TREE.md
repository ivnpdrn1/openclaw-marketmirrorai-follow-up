# EXIT Decision Tree



Purpose:



Define when MarketMirrorAI must recommend exiting an active thesis.



---



EXIT Conditions:



- Original visual pattern fails.

- Support breaks after CALL thesis.

- Resistance breaks after PUT thesis.

- Gap floor fails after bullish gap thesis.

- Trendline confirmation reverses.

- Volume contradicts original direction.

- Timeframes turn against the position.

- SPY context reverses strongly.

- Major catalyst invalidates original setup.

- Risk level exceeds acceptable threshold.



---



Operational Rule:



EXIT is not emotional.



EXIT occurs when the original thesis is no longer valid.



---



Final Decision



EXIT



Protect capital when the original diagnosis has been invalidated.
