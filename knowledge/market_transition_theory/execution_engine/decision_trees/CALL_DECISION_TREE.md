# CALL Decision Tree



Purpose:



Define how bullish opportunities are approved.



---



Step 1



Visual Diagnosis



Question:



Was a bullish visual structure detected?



Examples:



- MMAI-001 Piso Fuerte

- MMAI-002 Primer Gap al Alza



If NO:



Decision = WAIT



---



Step 2



Rule Validation



Were required rules satisfied?



If NO:



Decision = WAIT



---



Step 3



Volume Confirmation



Is volume supportive?



If NO:



Reduce confidence.



---



Step 4



Timeframe Alignment



Does the hourly timeframe agree?



Does the daily timeframe agree?



If NO:



Decision = WAIT



---



Step 5



Market Context



SPY trend supportive?



Major bearish catalyst active?



If contradictory:



Decision = WAIT



---



Step 6



Pattern Dependencies



Does the setup have confirmation patterns?



Example:



MMAI-001

+

MMAI-002



If YES:



Increase confidence.



---



Final Decision



CALL



Only when visual evidence, rules, context, and confirmations agree.
