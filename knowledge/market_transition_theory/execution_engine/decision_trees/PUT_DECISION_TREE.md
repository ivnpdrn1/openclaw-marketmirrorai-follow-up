# PUT Decision Tree



Purpose:



Define how bearish opportunities are approved.



---



Step 1



Visual Diagnosis



Question:



Was a bearish visual structure detected?



Examples:



- MMAI-005 Canal Bajista

- MMAI-007 Primera Vela Roja de Apertura

- MMAI-008 Ruptura del Piso del Gap



If NO:



Decision = WAIT



---



Step 2



Rule Validation



Were required rules satisfied?



If NO:



Decision = WAIT



---



Step 3



Volume Confirmation



Is volume supportive of downside movement?



If NO:



Reduce confidence.



---



Step 4



Timeframe Alignment



Does the lower timeframe agree with the higher timeframe?



If NO:



Decision = WAIT



---



Step 5



Market Context



SPY trend bearish or weak?



Major bullish catalyst active?



If contradictory:



Decision = WAIT



---



Step 6



Pattern Dependencies



Does the setup have confirmation patterns?



Example:



MMAI-005

+

MMAI-007

+

MMAI-008



If YES:



Increase confidence.



---



Final Decision



PUT



Only when bearish visual evidence, rules, context, and confirmations agree.
