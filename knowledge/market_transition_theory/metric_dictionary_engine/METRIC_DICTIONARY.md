# MarketMirrorAI Metric Dictionary



## t1



Definition:



Time between BUY Decision Point and actual trend change.



Meaning:



Measures anticipation quality of entries.



Optimization Direction:



Higher is better.



Interpretation:



A larger t1 indicates earlier detection of trend changes.



---



## t2



Definition:



Time between actual trend change and actual opposite trend change.



Meaning:



Measures opportunity duration.



Optimization Direction:



Higher is better.



Interpretation:



Longer trends create larger profit opportunities.



---



## t3



Definition:



Time between SELL Decision Point and actual opposite trend change.



Meaning:



Measures anticipation quality of exits.



Optimization Direction:



Higher is better.



Interpretation:



Larger t3 indicates earlier detection of trend exhaustion.



---



## h



Definition:



Total magnitude of the trend.



Meaning:



Measures total economic opportunity.



Optimization Direction:



Higher is better.



Interpretation:



Larger trends create larger profit potential.



---



## Capture Efficiency



Definition:



Captured h / Total h



Meaning:



Measures how much of the available opportunity was captured.



Optimization Direction:



Higher is better.



---



## Ratio1



Definition:



Correctly Anticipated Trend Changes / Total Decision Points



Meaning:



Measures diagnostic accuracy.



Optimization Direction:



Higher is better.



---



## Ratio2



Definition:



Decision Points Without Trend Change / Total Decision Points



Meaning:



Measures false signal rate.



Optimization Direction:



Lower is better.



---



## Opportunity Ranking Accuracy (ORA)



Definition:



Ranking quality of selected symbols versus all available opportunities.



Meaning:



Measures asset selection quality.



Optimization Direction:



Higher is better.



---



## WAIT Accuracy



Definition:



Correct WAIT decisions / Total WAIT decisions.



Meaning:



Measures quality of inactivity.



Optimization Direction:



Higher is better.



---



## Pattern Decay Rate



Definition:



Loss of effectiveness over time.



Meaning:



Measures pattern degradation.



Optimization Direction:



Lower is better.



---



## Confidence Calibration



Definition:



Alignment between predicted confidence and actual outcomes.



Meaning:



Measures confidence reliability.



Optimization Direction:



Higher is better.



---



## Signal Efficiency Ratio (SER)



Definition:



Profit Generated / Time Exposed



Meaning:



Measures productivity of capital exposure.



Optimization Direction:



Higher is better.



---



## Opportunity Miss Score



Definition:



Missed profitable opportunities.



Meaning:



Measures failure to participate in valid trends.



Optimization Direction:



Lower is better.



---



## Evolution Velocity



Definition:



Rate of improvement across review periods.



Meaning:



Measures learning speed.



Optimization Direction:



Higher is better.
