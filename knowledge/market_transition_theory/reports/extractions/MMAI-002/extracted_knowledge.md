# MMAI-002 Extracted Knowledge



Source:



CREA TU PROPIA RIQUEZA



Chapter:



Estrategia del Primer Gap al Alza



Core Thesis:



The first bullish gap after a significant decline and near a strong support zone is often the first signal of trend reversal.



Required Conditions:



* Significant prior decline

* Strong support zone nearby

* First candle must be green

* Gap floor must hold all day

* Hourly timeframe analysis

* Above-average volume

* Strong bullish candle structure

* End-of-day confirmation



Expected Outcome:



* Recovery of prior decline

* Potential continuation higher

* Typical duration:



  * 2–3 days

  * Up to one week



Risk Factors:



* False gaps

* Gap floor violation

* Weak volume

* Improper candle structure
