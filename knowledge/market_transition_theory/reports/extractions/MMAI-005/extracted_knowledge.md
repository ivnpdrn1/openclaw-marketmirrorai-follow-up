# MMAI-005 Extracted Knowledge



Source:



CREA TU PROPIA RIQUEZA



Chapter:



2. Estrategia del Canal Bajista



Core Thesis:



Buying inside a confirmed bearish channel is discouraged.



The preferred action is to wait until price breaks the channel ceiling.



Definition:



A Canal Bajista is formed by connecting descending highs and projecting a parallel lower boundary.



Primary Timeframe:



Hourly



Visual Requirements:



- Two descending highs



- Downward ceiling



- Parallel floor



- Price movement inside channel



Required Conditions:



- Confirmed bearish channel



- Hourly timeframe



- Descending ceiling



- Valid channel structure



Entry Trigger:



- Breakout above channel ceiling



- Green candle



- Preferably after 11:00 AM



Expected Outcome:



- Bullish continuation



- Increased CALL profitability



Forbidden Action:



- Buying CALLs inside the channel



Operational Importance:



MMAI-005 is a transition structure.



Its primary purpose is to identify environments where waiting is preferable until breakout occurs.
