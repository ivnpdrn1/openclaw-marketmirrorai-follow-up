Source:



CREA TU PROPIA RIQUEZA



Chapter:



4. Estrategia de Piso Fuerte - SPY



Core Thesis:



A strong support zone in the daily timeframe becomes a high-probability opportunity when the hourly chart confirms a reversal through a bullish breakout.



Definition of Piso Fuerte:



A price level visited and respected two or more times over a period of at least six months.



Author Observation:



For SPY, the 100-day moving average frequently acts as a Piso Fuerte.



Multi-Timeframe Structure:



Daily Timeframe:



- Identify the Piso Fuerte zone.

- Identify major support.

- Identify the magnitude of the decline.



Hourly Timeframe:



- Draw the descending trendline.

- Wait for bullish breakout.

- Require green candle.

- Prefer confirmation after 11:00 AM.

- Require above-average volume.



Required Conditions:



- Prior decline exists.

- Price near Piso Fuerte.

- Descending trendline exists.

- Trendline breakout occurs.

- Green candle confirms breakout.

- Volume exceeds average.

- Hourly timeframe confirms entry.



Expected Outcome:



- Rapid bullish reaction.

- Recovery of prior decline.

- High reward-to-risk opportunities.



Performance Claims:



- Typical large percentage returns.

- Example shown with SPY.

- Example shown with Facebook.



Operational Importance:



This is the primary CALL setup in the methodology.
