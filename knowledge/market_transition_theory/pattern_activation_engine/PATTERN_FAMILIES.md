# MMAI Pattern Families



Purpose:



Group MMAI patterns into operational families.



Pattern families determine which patterns are activated during initial diagnosis.



---



## Bullish Reversal Family



Purpose:



Detect potential CALL opportunities after decline, support, or bullish transition.



Activated By:



- Strong support detected

- Piso Fuerte detected

- Bullish gap detected

- Price rejection at support

- Bullish reversal candle



Patterns:



- MMAI-001 Piso Fuerte SPY

- MMAI-002 Primer Gap al Alza

- MMAI-003 Gap Normal al Alza

- MMAI-004 Gap Bajista al Alza



Primary Decision:



CALL



---



## Bearish Continuation Family



Purpose:



Detect potential PUT opportunities during bearish structure or downside continuation.



Activated By:



- Descending channel detected

- Lower highs detected

- Lower lows detected

- Bearish opening pressure

- Support failure

- Gap floor failure



Patterns:



- MMAI-005 Canal Bajista

- MMAI-006 Caída Regular y Caída Fuerte

- MMAI-007 Primera Vela Roja de Apertura

- MMAI-008 Ruptura del Piso del Gap



Primary Decision:



PUT



---



## Event Family



Purpose:



Detect opportunities or risks related to earnings and post-earnings behavior.



Activated By:



- Earnings date near

- Earnings report released

- Post-earnings gap

- Abnormal post-earnings volume

- Strong reaction after report



Patterns:



- MMAI-009 Earnings Report

- MMAI-010 Post-Earning



Primary Decision:



Context Dependent



---



## Confirmation Family



Purpose:



Confirm, reject, or reduce confidence in primary patterns.



Activated By:



- Any CALL candidate

- Any PUT candidate

- Any unclear visual diagnosis



Patterns:



- MMAI-011 Promedio Móvil 40H

- MMAI-012 Soporte y Resistencia

- MMAI-013 Entrada Perfecta y MACD

- MMAI-014 Volumen

- MMAI-015 Marcos de Tiempo



Primary Decision:



Confirmation Layer



---



Operational Rule:



Primary families generate candidate direction.



Confirmation families validate or reject the candidate.



Event family may override or reduce confidence.



When family signals conflict, decision must move toward WAIT.
