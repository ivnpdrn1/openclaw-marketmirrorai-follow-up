# MMAI-008 Rules



Pattern:



Ruptura del Piso del Gap



Rule 1



A prior gap must exist.



Rule 2



Price must fail to hold the lower boundary of the gap.



Rule 3



Breakdown should occur with selling pressure.



Rule 4



Volume should increase during the breakdown.



Rule 5



SPY should be neutral or bearish.



Rule 6



No major bullish reversal confirmation should be present.



Decision



If Rules 1–6 are satisfied, classify as bearish gap failure candidate.
