# MMAI-015 Rules



Pattern:



Marcos de Tiempo



Rule 1



Every signal must be evaluated in multiple timeframes.



Rule 2



Higher timeframes have priority over lower timeframes.



Rule 3



Conflicting timeframes reduce confidence.



Rule 4



Aligned timeframes increase confidence.



Rule 5



No trading decision should rely on a single timeframe.



Decision



Use as context and confidence layer.
