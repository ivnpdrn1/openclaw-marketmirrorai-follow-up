# MMAI-014 Rules



Pattern:



Volumen



Rule 1



Volume must be compared against recent average volume.



Rule 2



Bullish signals are stronger when price rises with increasing volume.



Rule 3



Bearish signals are stronger when price falls with increasing volume.



Rule 4



Low volume reduces signal confidence.



Rule 5



Volume confirmation must not be used alone.



Decision



Use as confirmation or rejection layer for CALL and PUT signals.



Status



Research
