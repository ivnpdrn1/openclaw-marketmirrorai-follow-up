# MMAI-002 Rules



Pattern:



Primer Gap al Alza



Source Basis:



CREA TU PROPIA RIQUEZA

- Estrategia del Primer Gap al Alza

- OCR lines approximately 2628-2810



Rule 1



The setup must occur after an important prior decline.



Rule 2



Price should be near a strong support zone or strong floor.



Rule 3



The first candle of the gap must be green.



Rule 4



After the first green candle is complete, the gap floor must be identified.



Rule 5



Price must respect the gap floor for the entire day.



Rule 6



The setup should be evaluated in the hourly timeframe.



Rule 7



The bullish candle with volume must be well formed.



Rule 8



Volume should be above normal average volume.



Rule 9



Avoid candles with long upper tails or inverted hammer structure.



Rule 10



Entry should preferably occur near the end of the trading session after confirming that the gap floor was not broken.



Decision



If Rules 1-10 are satisfied, classify as high-quality bullish transition candidate.
