# MMAI-011 Rules



Pattern:



Promedio Móvil 40H



Rule 1



Price must be evaluated relative to the 40-hour moving average.



Rule 2



Price above the moving average favors bullish bias.



Rule 3



Price below the moving average favors bearish bias.



Rule 4



Moving average slope should be considered.



Rule 5



Moving average should not be used in isolation.



Decision



Use as trend confirmation layer.



Status



Research
